# Terms of Service

**SubStation** is provided as-is, without warranty of any kind.

## Usage

- SubStation is a moderation coordination tool. It does not take automated moderation actions (removals, bans) on its own.
- AI features (pattern radar, post-mortems) require moderators to provide their own API keys. SubStation does not include or provide API keys.
- AI-generated content (post-mortems, pattern analysis) is advisory. Moderators are responsible for all moderation decisions.

## Limitations

- SubStation stores data only within Devvit's Redis, scoped to your subreddit installation.
- The app depends on Reddit's Devvit platform. Availability is subject to Reddit's infrastructure.
- AI features depend on third-party providers (OpenAI, Google Gemini). Their terms of service apply to API usage.
